About Hub City Yoga To-Go

Philosophy

Slow, breath-led practices inspired by nature’s cycles—think forest greens and warm earth tones, now reflected in our new palette.

Who it’s for

Beginners, busy professionals, and anyone seeking grounding movement and mindful rest. Mat-friendly options for all bodies.

Where we meet

Homes, offices, parks, and community spaces around Spartanburg. We bring the props—you bring yourself.

Offerings

Private Session

  • 60–75 minutes tailored to your goals
  • Breath, mobility, and gentle strength
  • Great for new or returning students

Small Group

  • Friends, family, or coworkers (up to 8)
  • Calming pace with options for all
  • Perfect for weekly routines

Restorative / Chair

  • Deep rest with props or chair support
  • Nervous system-friendly pacing
  • Ideal for recovery and relaxation
